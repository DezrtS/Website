Ice Shard Mechanic

In Fenris, I implemented the player's ice shard attack. The ice shard acts as both something to damage enemies as well as an aid for movement. When the ice shard flys over a body of water, it freezes the water, allowing the player to cross over otherwise impossible gaps.

Dynamic Enemy AI

I designed and implemented the AI for every type of enemy in Fenris. There are 6 different enemies the player can encounter, ranging from the Ice Wolf, an aggressive creature that bites and conjoures ice shards, to the Frozen Skeleton, a melee focused creature that attacks with alternating hands, to the Boulder, a creature made up of rocks that attacks with AOE and rolling based attacks, to the Shard Thrower, a purely ranged enemy that hurls ice shards at the player.
