About Me

Beyond the code and game development, I am a creative person at heart who loves to learn and try new things.

I started making games when I was 15. It all starting in a computer science class when I made my first ever game called Survive "Survival". Ever since then, that passion to create games has grown into my true purpose. There is no greater feeling then seeing people play and enjoy the games I make.
